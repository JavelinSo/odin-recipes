Boneless Wings Are the Superior Wings

There is a tradition in my home, that sunday is for football. And Football Sunday is for fried food. That means boneless wings.

Why boneless? Because Americans have been bamboozeled by a criminal comspiracy perpetrated by the commercial chicken empire to settle for the left-over chicken parts nobody wants. Americans have been told wings and drumsticks are worth the terrible work-reward ratio. (some scientists have put the number near 459:1) No more my friends. This is America, and by all the Gods I say Boneless Wings are the Superior Wing.

Follow this recipe to the promised land.

Get Your Act Together

  • 3 pounds boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 packages Zatarains buttermilk chicken breading
  • Lawry's season salt
  • Garlic
  • Pepper
  • 4 eggs

Whatcha Gonna Did

  1. First, cut up that chicken into wing sized pieces
  2. Next whisk up them eggs
  3. Get your Zatarain's, dump it in a big bowl, add the other seasonings as you see fit, You know what you like
  4. Don't mess around. Kick off is in 45 minutes. It's go time
  5. Mix all your chicken in with the eggs. Make sure everythings coated real good
  6. Now bread all that chicken with that sweet Zatarain's mix you got going
  7. Did you remember to turn the Fryer on? 375 degrees. Get on it Rookie!
  8. Fry them babies up
  9. Like Grandpa always said, "When it floats, it's done"

I highly recommend, if not mandate, you toss these in the wings sauce of your choice, and serve these with some freshy cut Freedom Fries, a cold adult beverage, and enjoy that delish 1:1 work-reward ratio.
