The Whiskey Sour

Cooking is often exhausting work. Sometimes you need a quality refreshment.

The whiskey sour is a classic cocktail, best after a good steak.

And please. Use a good Bourbon.

Assemble the parts

  • Bourbon
  • Lemon juice
  • Simple Syrup
  • One small egg white (trust me)
  • Ice

Get it together

  1. Add burbon, lemon juice, simple syrup, and egg white to a cocktail shaker. NO ICE YET
  2. Seal up the shaker, and shake for about 10 seconds
  3. Now, add the ice to the shaker and shake another 10 seconds
  4. Strain this beautiful nectar of the gods into a rocks glass
  5. Garnish with a cherry, or don't
  6. Enjoy
