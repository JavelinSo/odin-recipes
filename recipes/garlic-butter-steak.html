Garlic Butter Steak

A must have in every man's cookbook

Every man should know how to cook a good steak. While the prefered method is open flame, that's not always possible.

When conditions don't allow an open flame, pan searing in a large cast iron skillet is the next best thing. Let's get cooking.

Ingredients
  • Steak. prefreably a top sirloin
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon minced fresh parsley
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Tha's it. Don't get in the way of a good steak by adding a bunch of extras.

Sizzle it up
  1. Mix 1 tablespoon butter with parsley, garlic
  2. Sprinkle steak with salt and pepper
  3. Heat remaining butter in cast iron skillet over medium heat
  4. Add steak, cook until desired doneness

When contemplating how your steak is cooked, the thermometer should read:

  • 135 for medium-rare
  • 140 for meduim
  • 145 for medium-well

Anything with a temp higher than 155 should be considered overcooked and fed to the dog
