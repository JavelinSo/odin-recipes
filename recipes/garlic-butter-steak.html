Garlic Butter Steak

A must have in every man's cookbook

Every man should know how to cook a good steak. While the prefered method is open flame, that's not always possible.

When conditions don't allow an open flame, pan searing in a large cast iron skillet is the next best thing. Let's get cooking.

Ingredients
  • Steak. prefreably a top sirloin
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon minced fresh parsley
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Tha's it. Don't get in the way of a good steak by adding a bunch of extra's.

Sizzle it up
    Mix 1 tablespoon butter with parsley, garlic
    Sprinkle steak with salt and pepper
    Heat remaining butter in cast iron skillet over medium heat
    Add steak, cook until disired donenes
